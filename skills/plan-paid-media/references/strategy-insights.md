# Paid Media Strategy Insights

## Diagnose the system, not the platform screen

Evaluate creative, placement, audience or intent, first-party data, landing experience, offer, sales follow-up, and economics together. Automated campaign products reduce manual control but increase the need for clean goals, conversion data, creative variety, and downstream quality feedback.

## Brand and performance

Treat performance media as a testing and demand-capture system, not proof of incrementality. Track both business outcomes and intermediate movement: qualified reach, meaningful engagement, consideration, valid lead, sale, margin, retention, and brand search where appropriate.

## Budget decisions

Move budget only after checking measurement consistency, marginal cost, audience overlap, creative fatigue, lead quality, refund or cancellation, and operational capacity. Avoid fixed scaling percentages and universal platform benchmarks.

Any named platform product, audience model, attribution feature, eligibility rule, or learning behavior must be verified in current official documentation before use.
