# Paid-media operations

## Pre-launch

- Eligibility and industry documentation
- Billing, permissions, asset ownership
- Conversion event and CRM feedback
- Naming, UTM, exclusions, brand safety
- Mobile landing path, forms, phone, chat, payment
- Test matrix, budget guardrail, stop rule

## Diagnosis

- No delivery: review, balance, budget, bid, audience/query volume, asset state
- Low CTR: intent/audience, promise, title/first frame, placement, fatigue
- Low CVR: traffic quality, message continuity, speed, trust, price, form, support
- Low sales: lead definition, duplicates, response speed, sales process, offer, fit
- Low ROI: margin, AOV, CVR, CAC, refunds, repeat purchase, attribution window

## Review output

Goal | spend | delivery | audience/query | creative | page | valid leads | sales | economics | decision
