# Incident Patterns

## Promotion and pricing incidents

Verify the displayed price, promotion rules, coupon interaction, transaction records, time window, affected users, legal obligations, and operational cause. Align customer service, platform, executives, legal, and media on one fact base. Design remedy around actual harm and publish corrective controls; do not use compensation to replace fact finding.

## Rumor and false-information incidents

Preserve original posts, timestamps, reach, edits, source chain, and affected-party evidence. Distinguish false statement, mistaken inference, satire, opinion, impersonation, and coordinated manipulation. Choose among clarification, platform report, direct response, stakeholder outreach, legal preservation, and no-response monitoring based on harm and amplification risk.

## Timing

Do not apply a universal “golden hours” rule. Respond as soon as the minimum verified fact base and decision authority are ready. When verification will take time, issue a holding update that states what is known, what is being checked, immediate protective action, and the next update time.
