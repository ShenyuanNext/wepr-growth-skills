# Execution checklists

## Intake

- Objective, deadline, authority, budget, jurisdictions
- Confirmed facts and evidence owner
- First source, amplification nodes, search footprint
- Affected people and material harm
- Legal, regulatory, employee, customer, investor, and partner exposure

## Holding statement

- What happened or what is being verified
- Who is affected
- Immediate action
- Contact or support path
- Next update time

## Media preparation

- Reporter, outlet, topic, format, deadline, quotation rules
- Three core messages
- Ten hostile questions
- Known unknowns and non-answer boundary
- Evidence pack and follow-up owner

## Postmortem

- Detection, escalation, approval, wording, execution, monitoring
- What amplified or reduced harm
- Process owner and preventive change
