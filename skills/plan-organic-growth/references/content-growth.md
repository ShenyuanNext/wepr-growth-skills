# Content growth system

## Sources

Customer questions, sales objections, community language, search demand, product data, project postmortems, competitive gaps, and industry change.

## Structure

Problem or hook → concrete evidence → upgraded understanding → method or steps → executable checklist → limits → natural next action.

## Distribution

- Xiaohongshu: searchable value, trust, saves, comment questions, and in-platform paths.
- X/Twitter: clear viewpoints, threads, relationships, real-time discussion, and repeated expertise.
- LinkedIn: professional problems, founder insight, cases, company credibility, and sales conversations.
- Instagram: recognizable visual systems, carousels, series, and consistent publishing.
- Short video: quick demonstration, native delivery, continued attention, and verifiable action.

Preserve core facts but rewrite opening, format, proof, interaction, and call-to-action for each platform. After a breakout post, expand related content, profile assets, search capture, subscription, and product paths instead of chasing only the next spike.
