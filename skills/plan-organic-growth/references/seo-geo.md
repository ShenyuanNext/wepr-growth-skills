# SEO and GEO execution

## SEO

Treat SEO as three connected systems:

- Structure: crawl, indexation, canonicalization, rendering, speed, architecture, URLs, internal links, multilingual handling.
- Content: search intent, topic maps, product-adjacent questions, comparisons, cases, useful answers, updates, consolidation.
- Trust: authors, company facts, methods, evidence, customer proof, relevant editorial mentions, digital PR, genuine community references.

Measure indexation, visibility, qualified traffic, inquiries, revenue quality, and content reuse—not page or keyword count alone.

## GEO / AI search

1. Define brand, category, comparison, scenario, and decision prompts.
2. Record baseline mentions, accuracy, competitors, citations, and missing facts across target AI products.
3. Audit entity consistency, source authority, AI-crawler access, structured data, and citation-friendly passages.
4. Build stable fact, product, method, FAQ, case, comparison, research, and expert pages.
5. Add third-party validation through relevant media, communities, partners, directories, and research citations.
6. Track mentions, citations, source pages, answer accuracy, brand search, referred visits, inquiries, and correction time.

Do not claim that llms.txt, schema, backlinks, Reddit, or any single tactic guarantees AI inclusion.
