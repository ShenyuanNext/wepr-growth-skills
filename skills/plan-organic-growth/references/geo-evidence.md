# GEO Evidence System

GEO should improve the probability that a brand's accurate information can be discovered, understood, corroborated, and cited. It cannot guarantee model output.

## Baseline

Build a representative question set across brand, category, scenario, comparison, purchase, risk, support, and industry topics. Record model, interface, date, geography, login state, prompt, answer, sources, brand mention, accuracy, and reproducibility.

## Evidence assets

Create direct answers supported by product facts, dated research, author identity, methods, cases with limitations, FAQs, technical documentation, and machine-readable structure where appropriate. Strengthen entity consistency across owned and credible independent sources.

## Measurement

Track answer accuracy, qualified mention rate, citation rate and quality, source diversity, sentiment or framing, branded demand, assisted visits, and business outcomes. Do not use fixed platform-source hierarchies or market forecasts without current evidence; citation behavior changes by model, mode, query, and time.
