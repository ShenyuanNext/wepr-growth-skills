# SEO System

Treat SEO as a system connecting search demand, page usefulness, crawlability, information architecture, evidence, reputation, links, and business outcomes.

## Execution sequence

1. Define search intent and business value;
2. Inspect current results and alternative answers;
3. Choose the page type and unique information gain;
4. Build content and internal-link architecture;
5. Verify crawl, render, canonical, index, mobile, performance, and structured data;
6. Publish, inspect indexing and queries, improve usefulness, and measure downstream outcomes;
7. Earn relevant mentions and links through products, research, tools, partnerships, and digital PR.

Third-party authority metrics are comparative vendor metrics, not search-engine scores. A directory's domain rating does not make a submission useful. Verify platform relevance, editorial quality, link behavior, index status, referral potential, policy, and current availability before recommending it.
