# Core Account Launch System

## Select a platform by operating fit

| Need | Strong fit when |
| --- | --- |
| WeChat Official Account | The business can sustain deeper articles, subscriptions, archives, and relationship-based distribution |
| Xiaohongshu | The audience searches, compares, saves, and uses visual proof for lifestyle or purchase decisions |
| Douyin | The value can be demonstrated, narrated, or dramatized quickly through native video |
| WeChat Channels | Video can connect naturally with an existing WeChat audience, brand ecosystem, service, shop, or live operation |
| X/Twitter | The operator can contribute timely ideas, work-in-public notes, replies, and professional network value |

Choose from audience behavior, evidence format, production capacity, buying process, and permitted conversion path. Do not choose only from reported reach.

## Launch evidence pack

Before building a calendar, assemble:

- 20 or more raw units when available: questions, objections, cases, screenshots, work steps, demonstrations, research notes, and stories;
- proof and permission status for every customer, image, quotation, and result;
- account promise, three to five pillars, and a clear non-goal list;
- profile copy, pinned-content plan, content brief, and review sheet.

Counts are planning aids, not algorithm requirements. Start with fewer units when evidence quality is higher or production is constrained.

## Experiment design

Use the account's own baseline. Compare like with like and avoid changing topic, hook, format, length, proof, CTA, and publishing context all at once. Record data definitions and capture time. Small samples support learning hypotheses, not causal certainty.

## Business measurement

Track qualified audience, repeated questions, profile behavior, conversations, lead quality, sales progress, retention, and reusable knowledge assets alongside platform metrics. A high-view item with wrong-audience engagement can be a negative result.
