# WeChat Official Account Launch

## Account foundation

Align name, avatar, description, automatic reply, menu, recurring opener, article series, and next action with one reader promise. Prepare article briefs and source materials before committing to a publishing frequency.

## Article jobs

- Search or utility: answer a durable question with verified detail.
- Trust: reveal method, judgment, evidence, and limitations.
- Story: make a real decision, conflict, or consequence legible.
- Proof: show process, cases, research, or comparison with permission.
- Conversion: explain fit, process, FAQ, and a permitted next step.
- Retention: build recurring columns, internal links, and an archive readers can navigate.

Use `$create-marketing-content` for drafting. Write a brief first: reader, job, primary promise, source ledger, title options, opening, sections, evidence, CTA, and risk check.

## Diagnosis

Review distribution eligibility, topic-reader fit, title and preview, opening, completion, saves or shares, follows, downstream actions, and production quality in order. Do not infer a recommendation pool, fixed publication time, or monetization outcome from anecdotes. Verify current rules and eligibility in official WeChat sources.
