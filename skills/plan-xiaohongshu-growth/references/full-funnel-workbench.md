# Xiaohongshu Full-Funnel Workbench

## Route by the earliest broken stage

| Stage | Diagnostic question | Primary skill |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Account promise | Is the target reader and recurring value clear? | `$launch-content-account` |
| Profile acceptance | Does the profile confirm identity, proof, and next step? | `$xiaohongshu-profile` |
| Topic relevance | Do topics match real situations, searches, and decisions? | `$xiaohongshu-topic-planner` |
| Packaging | Does the title create a truthful reason to open? | `$xiaohongshu-title` |
| Content delivery | Does the body fulfill the promise with execution value? | `$plan-xiaohongshu-growth` |
| Conversation | Do replies extend value and handle objections naturally? | `$xiaohongshu-comment-reply` |
| Conversion | Is the next step permitted, specific, and operationally supported? | `$xiaohongshu-conversion-path` |

Do not repair a later stage while an earlier stage remains unclear. Strong titles cannot compensate for an incoherent account promise, and more calls to action cannot compensate for missing trust.

## Full-post handoff

When another skill passes a topic into `$plan-xiaohongshu-growth`, preserve:

- target reader and current situation;
- primary content job and search intent;
- primary keyword and supporting language;
- title or cover promise;
- proof and material available;
- desired action and compliance boundary;
- observation or learning goal.

Return the final post, title options if needed, keyword notes, save-worthy checklist, natural closing, and risk check. Do not repeat a full profile or conversion audit unless it materially blocks the copy.

## Review loop

Review the publishing path by stage: eligibility and exposure → open → reading depth → saves/comments by motive → profile visits → follows → relevant conversations → qualification → business outcome. Use the account's own baseline and comparable formats. Record qualitative evidence such as questions, objections, sharing reasons, and non-action reasons.
