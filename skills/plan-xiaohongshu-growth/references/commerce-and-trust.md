# Xiaohongshu Commerce and Trust

Low follower count does not prove low commercial value. Diagnose the complete path: qualified discovery → content trust → product fit → objection removal → transaction → fulfillment → service → repeat or referral.

## Search-to-conversion audit

- Does the topic match an active problem or decision?
- Does the content answer the query without hiding the useful part?
- Are product claims demonstrated and attributable?
- Are price, delivery, return, suitability, and support objections addressed?
- Is the purchase or consultation path permitted, clear, and low-friction?
- Can operations deliver the promise consistently?

Do not use coordinated comments, fake ordinary users, copied materials, undisclosed commercial posts, coded off-platform diversion, sensitive-word substitution intended to evade moderation, or fixed posting-volume and account-warming rules. Re-verify current platform commerce, advertising, disclosure, and link policies.
