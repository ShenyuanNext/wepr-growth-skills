# Creator Playbook Output

## Recommended structure

1. Analysis question and sample boundary;
2. Account positioning and target audience;
3. Topic architecture and coverage gaps;
4. Packaging patterns: title, cover, opening;
5. Content structures and proof devices;
6. Visual and voice system;
7. CTA and comment insights;
8. Patterns worth adapting, with evidence strength;
9. Elements not to copy;
10. Two-to-four-week experiment plan.

For multiple accounts, compare them on the same dimensions. Do not rank accounts using raw engagement when audience size, publication date, format, or paid support differs.

Each recommended experiment must include a hypothesis, changed variable, expected early signal, business signal, review date, and stop/continue rule.
